Cafaholic

Get Coffee. Faster. Smarter.

Your nearest bar or coffee is just a click away from your Windows Phone. Just allow the app your current location info and it'll help you track down the nearest cafes and bars. Store them securely. Powered by Foursquare API and Microsoft Azure, searching for your morning coffee or a pint of beer was never so easy. Store your favorite cafe, check in with friends and much more. The App is FREE and the code is open source licensed under MIT License.
